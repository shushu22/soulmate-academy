Virtual Program · 3 Sections · 20 Modules

The Full Curriculum

Redesigned from the ground up by relationship coaches, psychologists and somatic practitioners. Each section builds on the last — creating structural change, not just insight. Two live sessions per week, always recorded.

Section 1 Communication, Self-Understanding & Shadow 4 Weeks · 8 Sessions
01

Authentic Communication

Navigate conflict, express needs without blame, decode emotional cues, and build a container of genuine safety for both partners.

02

Deep Intimacy Beyond the Physical

Redefine intimacy as emotional, spiritual and erotic connection. Cultivate mindful presence, harmonise desires, and dissolve the barriers to true closeness.

03

Shadow Work & Hidden Patterns

Identify unconscious beliefs and inherited behaviours shaping your relationship. Transform reactive patterns before they cause further rupture.

04

Giving & Receiving Feedback

Master the balance between honesty and care. Turn criticism into connection, and feedback into a profound act of love and respect.

05

Conflict as a Doorway

Use disagreement as a pathway to depth. Learn de-escalation, repair rituals, and how to fight in ways that bring you closer rather than further apart.

06

Navigating Life's Evolutions

Relationships transform through children, career changes, relocation, loss. Learn to evolve together rather than grow apart through life's biggest transitions.

07

Past Relationships as Teachers

Mine your history for gold. Understand how your relational past shapes your present, and use those patterns as a map toward conscious partnership.

08

Making Coaching & Therapy Work

Harness the full power of professional support. Set goals, track progress, and integrate insights so that every investment in growth creates lasting change.

Section 2 Your Love Amplified — Values, Identity & Core Beliefs 3 Weeks · 6 Sessions
09

Soulful Connection & Shared Identity

Explore love, spirituality, culture and individuality as the tapestry of your relationship. Honour difference as a source of richness rather than friction.

10

Your Relational Ecosystem

Cultivate a community that nourishes your partnership. Set healthy boundaries with family and friends, and navigate complex social dynamics as a team.

11

Taming Complex Emotions

Work with jealousy, insecurity, anger, dependency and resentment at their roots — not their symptoms. Develop true emotional mastery together.

12

Roles, Power & Equality

Examine how gender, expectation and imbalance shape your dynamic. Build a truly equitable partnership where both feel fully seen and valued.

13

Defining Your Love Structure

Explore the full spectrum of relational forms with curiosity and openness. Define your own conscious agreements rather than inheriting someone else's defaults.

14

Family, Children & Legacy

Navigate the deeply personal questions: children, fertility, adoption, parenting philosophy, and the kind of legacy — emotional and material — you want to create together.

Section 3 Beyond the Vows — Abundance, Adventure & Shared Vision 3 Weeks · 6 Sessions
15

Money, Wealth & Financial Partnership

Transform financial conversations from a source of tension to a foundation for security. Align on shared wealth, individual freedom, and a plan for abundance.

16

Career, Ambition & Shared Success

Navigate the interplay between individual ambitions and partnership goals. Prevent burnout, support each other's growth, and build a life you both find meaningful.

17

Health, Longevity & Vitality Together

Understand how wellbeing — physical, emotional, spiritual — is deeply relational. Create shared health rituals and a roadmap to a long, vital life together.

18

Adventure, Play & Shared Passions

Reignite the pleasure of being together. Transform individual interests into shared adventures and create the kind of life that neither of you could build alone.

19

Designing Your Life on Your Terms

Challenge conventional structures. Whether digital nomads, homesteaders or urban pioneers — build a life that reflects your values, not inherited expectations.

20

Your Shared Vision & Relationship Blueprint

The capstone: articulate your shared destiny. Set joint goals, design your relationship rituals, and walk away with a living document — your personal relationship blueprint.
